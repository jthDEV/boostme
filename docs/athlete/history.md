# Workout History

The **History** page gives you a complete view of your training over time.

## Session list

Browse all your past sessions — both completed and skipped. Each entry shows:

- Date and time
- Session type (plan session or WOD)
- Completion status
- Feeling rating you gave

You can sort and filter the list to find specific sessions.

## Session detail

Tap any session to see the full detail:

- All phases and blocks
- Exercises with the weights and reps you recorded
- Your feeling rating and any coach feedback

## Stats dashboard

The history page includes training statistics across three time ranges:

| Tab | Shows |
|---|---|
| **Month** | Sessions this calendar month |
| **Year** | Sessions this calendar year |
| **All** | Lifetime session count |

A visual bar chart shows your training frequency over time.

## Personal high scores

Track your best lifts and performances across all sessions. High scores are automatically calculated from the per-exercise weights you record during [feedback](feedback.md).

The high scores list auto-scrolls to keep you motivated.

## Monthly calendar

A calendar view highlights the days you trained. Tap any highlighted day to see the session detail inline — no need to leave the calendar view.
