# Chats

All of your conversations in boostME live on the **Chats** page, reachable from the sidebar. This includes chats with your coach, group training threads, and WOD discussions.

## Opening a chat

1. Open **Chats** in the sidebar.
2. Tap any row in the list to jump straight into that conversation.

Each chat row shows an avatar plus a prefix so you can tell conversations apart at a glance:

| Prefix | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Chat with {Name}** | A coach chat or direct message |
| **Group · {Group name}** | A group training chat |
| **WOD · {WOD title}** | A thread around a specific Daily WOD |

## Coach chats

Coach chats open on their own full-page view. From the chat page you get:

- The coach's portrait and name in the header
- The full conversation history
- A back arrow that returns you to the Chats list

There are three ways into a coach chat:

1. **Chats list** — tap the coach's row.
2. **Coach Discovery card** — tap **Open chat with {FirstName}** on a coach you're already linked to.
3. **Trial chat** — on a coach you haven't booked yet, tap **Try a trial chat with {FirstName}**. This lets you sample the coach's voice and style before you commit to a paid booking. Trial chats do not require a subscription.

!!! tip
    The Coach Discovery page is a pure profile browser — you no longer need to hunt for a "Chat" tab inside the coach card. Use **Chats** in the sidebar or the buttons directly on the coach's profile.

## In-session and post-session chat

During a workout, the [session page](sessions.md) includes a coach chat toggle — tap the coach avatar to open it without leaving your workout.

After the session, the [Feedback page](feedback.md) offers a short post-session chat for a quick wrap-up with your coach.

Both of these flows share the same coach chat thread that lives on the Chats page, so nothing is lost when you switch context.
