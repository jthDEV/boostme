# Training Plans

boostME offers three training tiers. You can access them from the **Plan** page.

## Training options

### 1. Free Individual Plan

An AI-generated personalized training plan based on your profile:

- **Duration**: 6–12 weeks
- **Tailored to**: your experience level, goals, available equipment, and weekly training frequency
- **Cost**: Free, unlimited plan generations

When you request a new plan, the AI considers your profile data and generates a structured program with phases (warmup, skill/strength, workout, cooldown) for each session.

### 2. Daily WOD & Leaderboard

Subscribe to daily community workouts published by accredited coaches. Each WOD includes a leaderboard so you can compare your performance with other athletes.

See [Daily WODs & Leaderboard](wods.md) for details.

### 3. Personal AI Coach

Link to a personal AI coach for customized training and real-time feedback during your sessions. The AI coach adapts to your progress and provides motivational guidance.

See [Coaches](coaches.md) for details.

## Working with your plan

### Generating a plan

1. Go to the **Plan** page.
2. Select **Free Individual Plan**.
3. The AI generates your plan. This may take a few seconds.
4. Review the plan overview showing the weekly structure.

### Activating a plan

Once generated, you can:

- **Activate** — Start following this plan. Your next session will be the first session of the plan.
- **Discard** — Reject the plan and generate a new one.

### Active plan status

Your home screen shows your active plan with:

- Current week and session number
- Overall progress through the plan
- A prompt to start your next session
