# Feedback & Results

After completing a session, the **Feedback** page lets you record how the workout went and log your results.

## Session summary

At the top you'll see a summary of the session you just completed, including the phases and blocks you worked through.

## How did it feel?

Rate your overall feeling with one of five options:

| Rating | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 😵‍💫 Too Heavy | The workout was too intense |
| 😣 Heavy But OK | Tough, but manageable |
| 💪 Just Fine | Good intensity, well balanced |
| 😌 A Bit Too Low | Could have pushed harder |
| 🥱 Boring | Way too easy |

This feedback helps the AI and your coach calibrate future sessions.

## Recording results

Each block in your session gets its own result entry form. The fields depend on the block format:

| Block format | What you record |
|---|---|
| **AMRAP** | Rounds completed + extra reps |
| **For Time** | Completion time (mm:ss) |
| **Straight (Skill/Strength)** | Weight used per exercise (kg) |
| **Max Weight / Max Reps / Max Calories** | The value you achieved |
| **EMOM** | Weight used (if applicable) |

### Per-exercise weights

For skill and strength blocks, you can record the **weight used for each exercise** individually. This feeds into your personal high scores visible in your [workout history](history.md).

## Coach reaction

If you're linked to a coach (AI or human), the feedback page includes:

- An **AI-generated reaction** based on your workout performance and feeling rating — personalized and motivational.
- A **chat section** where you can have a short post-session conversation with your coach about the workout. This thread is the same one you see on the [Chats page](chats.md).

!!! info
    The post-session chat is designed for a brief exchange (2–3 messages). If you raise a health concern, the conversation extends to address it properly.
