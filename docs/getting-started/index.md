# Getting Started

Welcome to boostME! This section walks you through creating your account and setting up your profile so you can start training.

## For Athletes

1. **[Registration & Login](registration.md)** — Create your account, verify your email, and log in.
2. **[Your Profile](profile.md)** — Set your age, experience level, goals, available equipment, and preferences.

Once your profile is set up, head to the [Athlete Guide](../athlete/index.md) to generate your first training plan.

## For Coaches

Coaches have a separate registration flow. See the [Coach Onboarding & Accreditation](../coach/onboarding.md) guide for the full process.
