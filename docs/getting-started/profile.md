# Your Profile

On your first login you'll complete an onboarding form. You can update all of these settings later from the **Profile** page.

## Personal information

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **First name / Last name** | Your real name |
| **City / Country** | Used for your display name on leaderboards |
| **Age** | Helps the AI tailor plan intensity |
| **Gender** | Used for exercise scaling recommendations |

## Display name

Your display name appears on leaderboards and in coach interactions. You can choose between:

- **Initials mode** — e.g. "J.T. @ Berlin, DE"
- **Full name** — e.g. "John Trainer @ Berlin, DE"

A live preview shows exactly how your name will appear.

!!! tip "Which account am I using?"
    Your display name also appears in the **Logged in as** panel at the top of the sidebar — handy if you juggle multiple accounts. When the sidebar is collapsed, the panel shrinks to a 2-letter initials chip with the full name on hover.

## Training experience

Select your experience level:

- **Beginner** — New to structured training
- **Intermediate** — Consistent training for 1–2 years
- **Advanced** — Multiple years of focused training

This directly affects the difficulty of AI-generated plans.

## Training goals

Choose what you want to focus on:

- Strength
- Conditioning / Endurance
- Weight loss
- Muscle building
- General fitness
- Sport-specific performance

## Available equipment

Select the equipment you have access to from 16 options (e.g. barbell, dumbbells, pull-up bar, rowing machine, kettlebells, etc.). This is found under the **Equipment & Settings** tab.

Your AI-generated training plans will only include exercises you can actually do with your available gear.

## Timer preferences

Also under **Equipment & Settings**:

| Setting | Description |
|---|---|
| **Ramp-up countdown** | Seconds before a timer starts (default: 5s). Gives you time to get in position. |
| **Counter voice** | Choose between a male (Stephen) or female (Tiffany) voice for audio announcements. |

## Timezone

boostME auto-detects your timezone from your browser. You can override it manually if needed. All session times and WOD schedules are displayed in your local time.
