# Registration & Login

## Creating your account

1. Open the boostME app and tap **Register**.
2. Enter your **email address** and choose a **password**.

    !!! info "Password requirements"
        Your password must meet all of the following:

        - At least **8 characters**
        - At least one **uppercase** letter
        - At least one **number**
        - At least one **special character** (e.g. `!@#$%`)

        A live checklist shows you which criteria are met as you type.

3. Enter your **first name** and **last name**.
4. Tap **Register**.

## Email verification

After registering you'll receive a **verification email** with a 6-digit code.

1. Check your inbox (and spam folder).
2. Enter the 6-digit code on the confirmation screen.
3. Once verified, you'll be redirected to the login page.

!!! tip
    If you didn't receive the code, tap **Resend code** to get a new one.

## Logging in

1. Enter your **email** and **password**.
2. Tap **Log in**.

On your very first login, you'll be taken to the **onboarding** screen to complete your profile. See [Your Profile](profile.md).

## Session expiry

Your login session stays active while you use the app. If your session expires (e.g. after a long idle period), you'll be redirected to the login page with a **"Session expired"** banner. Simply log in again to continue.

!!! tip
    If you leave the app open in a background tab, boostME automatically checks your session when you return. No need to manually refresh.
