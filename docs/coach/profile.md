# Profile Setup

Your coach profile is what athletes see when browsing the Coach Discovery page. A complete, compelling profile builds trust and attracts subscribers.

## Basic information

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Name** | Your full name (first + last) |
| **Email** | Your registered email address |
| **Phone** | Contact number (required for accreditation) |

## Profile image

Upload a professional photo of yourself. The image:

- Is automatically converted to **WebP** format for performance
- Is resized to a maximum of **800 x 800** pixels
- Has EXIF metadata stripped for privacy

Tap the image area on your profile page to upload or replace your photo.

## Introduction

A free-text field where you introduce yourself to potential athletes. This is the first thing they'll read on your profile card. Keep it concise and highlight what makes you unique as a coach.

## Experience

Describe your coaching background — years of experience, notable achievements, competition results, and coaching philosophy.

## Awards

List any awards, podium finishes, or notable achievements in your coaching or athletic career.

## Specialisms

Select the areas you specialize in from the available options:

- CrossFit
- Olympic Lifting
- Powerlifting
- Gymnastics
- Endurance
- HIIT
- Mobility
- Nutrition
- Bodybuilding
- Functional Fitness
- Rehab / Injury Prevention

You can select multiple specialisms.

## Certifications

Add your professional certifications from the supported providers:

| Provider | Levels |
|---|---|
| **CrossFit** | L1, L2, L3, L4 |
| **NASM** | National Academy of Sports Medicine |
| **ISSA** | International Sports Sciences Association |
| **ACE** | American Council on Exercise |
| **CFSC** | Certified Functional Strength Coach |
| **NSCA** | National Strength and Conditioning Association |
| **Other** | Free text for unlisted certifications |

Use the searchable dropdown to find and add certifications.

## Monthly fee

Set your coaching subscription price. Enter the amount and select the currency. This is displayed to athletes on your profile card and in Coach Discovery.
