# Onboarding & Accreditation

Before you can start coaching on boostME, you need to complete a 4-step accreditation process. This ensures quality and trust for athletes on the platform.

## The 4-step process

```
1. Registered → 2. Profile Complete → 3. Accreditation Pending → 4. Active
```

A visual progress bar on your dashboard shows your current status and what's left to do.

### Step 1: Registration

1. Open the boostME Coach app.
2. Tap **Register**.
3. Enter your **email**, **password**, and **name**.
4. Verify your email with the 6-digit code sent to your inbox.

Password requirements are the same as for athletes — see [Registration & Login](../getting-started/registration.md).

### Step 2: Complete your profile

After logging in for the first time, your dashboard shows a **requirements checklist**. You need to complete all items:

- [ ] Upload a **profile image**
- [ ] Add your **phone number**
- [ ] Add at least one **certification**
- [ ] Select your **specialisms**
- [ ] Write an **introduction**
- [ ] Set your **experience** description
- [ ] Set your **monthly fee**

See [Profile Setup](profile.md) for details on each field.

!!! tip
    Complete all checklist items to unlock the accreditation request button. You can see your progress at a glance on the dashboard.

### Step 3: Request accreditation

Once your profile is complete:

1. Tap **Request Accreditation** on your dashboard.
2. Your profile enters review status (**Accreditation Pending**).
3. The boostME team reviews your profile, certifications, and qualifications.

### Step 4: Active

Once approved, your status changes to **Active**. You can now:

- Appear in the athlete **Coach Discovery** page
- Accept link requests from athletes
- Publish daily WODs
- Edit athlete sessions
